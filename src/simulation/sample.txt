Root Cause (why previous attempts failed)
The r/h ratio was 0.556 — particle diameter exceeded the grid cell size. This is a fundamental FLIP stability violation, not a separation solver issue. When minDist > h:

Each grid cell has ≤1 particle → noisy P2G/G2P velocity transfer
Pressure solver produces incorrect corrections → fights the separation solver
No amount of separation iterations or hash rebuilds can fix grid-level noise
